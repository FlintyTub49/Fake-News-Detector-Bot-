Understanding the Data

Considering the vast amount of textual data available at our disposal, the first step, clearly, was to gain a better understanding of the available data first

The primary visualization which we used for exploring the distribution of the data was the Word Cloud. The Word Clouds thus obtained have been displayed below.

Real News

A Word Cloud was generated on the basis of the Real News. Certain words which are visibly larger in the Word Cloud are the most frequently occuring words, and hence have more weightage.

Fake News

Similarly, a Word Cloud from the collected Fake News was also generated. We can instantly notice the visible differences in the most commonly occurring words in the two categories of data
